FAQ's

What is the estimated budget?

It depends on the project scope and complexity. Once I know the details, I’ll share a clear quote.

What is the estimated Time?

I prefer More Time More Quality, but My most projects take 3–10 days. Timeline varies based on revisions and deliverables.

What About daily Updates?

Yes, I share regular progress updates through chat or email. You’ll always know where the project stands.

Software you use?

Mainly Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, and Figma. I use what fits best for the task.

How many revisions you give?

Usually 2–3 revisions are included. But I'm flexible and want you to be happy with the final result.

Do you source Assets?

I prioritize make assets from scratch to stay consistent. But Yes, I use licensed or free commercial use assets as well when needed.
